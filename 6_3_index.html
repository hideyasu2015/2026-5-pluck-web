High-pigment culinary matcha for cakes, cookies, croissants, and pralines. Heat-resistant colour formula available.

  • High chlorophyll — vivid baked colour
  • Strong umami base note
  • Recommended: Grade C1 / C2
  • Packaging: 5 kg / 25 kg bulk

Documents & Compliance
Export-ready documentation

Every shipment can be accompanied by the documentation your import team or customs broker requires. Documents are issued on a per-lot basis.

✓

FDA Prior Notice

US import registration for food products. Filed for every shipment to the United States.

✓

Pesticide Residue Test

Third-party lab analysis aligned with Japanese, EU, and US MRL standards. Report included per lot.

✓

Certificate of Origin

Issued by the Japan Chamber of Commerce. Specifies prefecture of origin (Uji / Nishio / Yame).

✓

Phytosanitary Certificate

Required by many importing countries. Issued by the Japanese Ministry of Agriculture (MAFF).

✓

Health Certificate

Confirms product safety and manufacturing standards at source. Available for EU and GCC markets.

✓

Organic / JAS Certified

Selected SKUs carry Japanese Agricultural Standard (JAS) organic certification. USDA NOP available on select lots.

✓

HACCP / ISO 22000

Manufacturing sites operate under HACCP principles. ISO 22000 certification documentation available on request.

✓

Halal Certification

Available for select product lines — please specify at time of inquiry for UAE, Malaysia, and Indonesia.

✓

Heavy Metals Analysis

Lead, cadmium, arsenic, and mercury tested to EU and Codex Alimentarius limits. Results provided per lot.

Need a specific document? Our export documentation team can advise on what is required for your country's import process. Please mention your destination country in the inquiry form below.
